More About Me

My name is Peter Rice and I am a Sports Journalism Major at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I’ve had a passion for sports all my life and I want to make a career in the sports industry! After graduating high school in 2018 I attended South Dakota State University for two years before transferring to UNL. While there, I was a student coach for the Jackrabbit football team where I traveled along taking game videos and entering stats along with other tasks. At Nebraska I have gained skills in several different fields within the sports industry. I look forward to taking more classes as I approach graduation and eventually start my professional career. When I’m not studying I am either cooking or watching whatever big sports game is on!
